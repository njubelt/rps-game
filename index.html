Guppy is a handsome and sweet boy

Guppy was adopted in October of 2018. He has been the number one good boy in the house ever since! He is very lazy and loves cuddling and treats. Oh, and head scratches!

Facts about Guppy

Guppy broke the tip of his tail before he was adopted
Guppy is allergic to chicken, and cannot have any food with chicken byproducts
Guppy was found living in a ditch. He is now the king of the house!
Guppy is extremely playful. His favorite toy is a stuffed pickle
Guppy is the best cat in the world. As a reward for being ranked #1, he should receive extra treats tonight. Additionally, he should get extra head scratches and cuddles for being a good boy. Also a piece of ham would be nice.
